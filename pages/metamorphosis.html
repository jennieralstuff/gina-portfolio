Metamorphosis

- January 2016 -

This work is inspired by the book Metamorphosis by Franz Kafka, as a reinterpretation of the process of metamorphosis through more ambiguous figures. The piece illustrates the surfacing of the ulterior state of being within an individual consumed by the absurd reality of endurance within a mundane lifestyle. The individual is constrained by the expectations placed on them by family, society, and their environment. Ultimately, causing the individual to desire to reveal their true state of being.
