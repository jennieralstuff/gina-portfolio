Work About CV

Global Ambassador

- March 2017 -

Welcoming international students to UC Davis

Design Brief

The University of California Davis’ Global Ambassador Mentorship Program has requested the creation of a brochure and poster that can be used to encourage international students to apply to become a mentee, and international and domestic students to join the program as a mentor.

Goal

The program’s overall goal is to provide a welcoming community for first year, exchange, or transfer international students to more easily adjust to the UC Davis community. Through the program mentors and mentees have the opportunity to enhance their college experience, share their cultural background, and to create lasting friendships.

Symbols

After completing various mind maps, I found the symbols of the friendship bracelet, chain, and knot to be the most compelling way to communicate the importance of lasting bonds within the international community of Global Ambassador.

Visual References

Friendship bracelets symbolize the interlocking and stable bonds between individuals. The bracelet is meant to be worn until it falls off, after which the wearer’s wish may be granted.

Prayer flags symbolize strength and compassion and similarly to friendship bracelets, as they fade over time the individual's wishes are said to be granted as they are lifted into the wind.

Flags' & Bracelets' Colours

The colors are used within the bracelets to represent various countries flags, such as Ireland, Greece, China, and the United Kingdom, to further indicate the interconnectivity of the international community within the program. I choose these specific country flag colors as they represented the most universal color pallette among country flags around the world, further tying into the mission of Global Ambassador to be a welcoming community for all individuals and cultures.
