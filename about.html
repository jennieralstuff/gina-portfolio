About Gina

Gina Laverone is a fourth year Economics and Art Studio double major and Global Studies minor at the University of California Davis.

The diversity of these areas of study have allowed her to intersect the three fields to inspire new creative works. Much of her work centers around social/political/economic issues, psychological exploration, and philosophical inquires. In her free time she enjoys photographing, reading, writing, and exploring new places.

In the future Gina wishes to pursue a career in Graphic Design, as well as contribute to campaigns and work centered on addressing human rights issues.
